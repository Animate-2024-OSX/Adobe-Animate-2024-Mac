## Overview

<a href="#">Grids for Instagram</a> brings the Instagram experience to Mac with an interface designed specifically for desktop use. Rather than cramming a mobile interface into a browser window, Grids reimagines Instagram browsing for larger screens, keyboard input, and Mac workflows.

The <a href="#">feed display</a> presents posts in grid layouts that utilize available screen space. See more content simultaneously than the single-column mobile view permits. Resize the window to show more or fewer columns. Click any post for expanded view with comments and details. This grid approach transforms Instagram browsing from scrolling to scanning.

<a href="#">Keyboard navigation</a> enables efficient browsing without touching the mouse. Arrow keys move between posts in the grid. Enter opens selected posts for detailed viewing. Spacebar likes posts quickly. These shortcuts make browsing through large amounts of content fast and comfortable.

The <a href="#">Stories viewer</a> displays Stories content in proper resolution on your Mac display. Watch Stories from followed accounts or the Explore section. Progress through Story sequences with keyboard or mouse. The desktop viewing experience shows Story content larger and clearer than phone screens.

<a href="#">Multiple account support</a> manages several Instagram accounts from one application. Switch between accounts with quick selection rather than logging out and in. Each account maintains its own feed position and viewing state. This multi-account capability serves social media managers and users with personal plus professional accounts.

The <a href="#">search functionality</a> finds accounts, hashtags, and locations efficiently. Search results display in organized sections. Save frequent searches for quick access. Browse hashtag feeds and location-tagged content. This search system makes discovering content straightforward.

<a href="#">Direct messages</a> provide access to Instagram DMs from your desktop. Read and respond to message conversations. Send messages to individuals or groups. The desktop messaging experience benefits from full keyboard typing rather than phone input.

The <a href="#">notification system</a> alerts you to Instagram activity. See new likes, comments, and follows. Receive DM notifications. Choose which notification types to enable. Stay current with Instagram activity without checking the app constantly.
